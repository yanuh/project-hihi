How to prevent fires?
Matches and lighters are dangerous, especially if they're around flammable objects.
You never know when you might cause a fire. Having a fire extinguisher in a household is a must to prevent any damage to the home or family.
Smoking indoors risks a higher chance of starting a house fire. It is better to smoke in the open air or avoid smoking.
Store oil, gas, etc. carefully and keep flammable objects away from children to avoid any accidents.
Always be cautious in kitches, especially if someone is cooking. Make sure to unplug and turn off all appliances once done with them.
